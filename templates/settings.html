How to get a Telegram Bot Token
  1. Start a chat with @BotFather on Telegram
  2. Send the command /newbot
  3. Follow the instructions to name your bot
  4. BotFather will give you a token that looks like 123456789:ABCDefGhIJKlmNoPQRsTUVwxyZ
  5. Copy this token and paste it in the field above
Important: Keep your token secure! Anyone with your token can control your bot.
